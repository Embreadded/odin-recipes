Roots Platter

This'll get you digging for more

Roots Platter is a cooked dish. You can prepare this dish in the kitchen of your home when you have the upgraded farmhouse.
This recipe is unlocked after reaching Level 3 in the Combat skill. It can also randomly be found in both the Volcano Dungeon shop and Krobus' shop on Saturdays.
It is also possible to find 5 Roots Platter in a treasure room in the Skull Cavern.

Ingredients
(1) Cave Carrot
(1) Winter Root

Information
Buff(s) Attack (+3)
Buff Duration 5m 35s
Energy 125
Health 56
Sell Price 100g
