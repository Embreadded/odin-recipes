Red Plate

Full of antioxidents

Red Plate is a cooked dish. You can prepare this dish in the kitchen of your home when you have the upgraded farmhouse.
This recipe is given by the Villager Emily, to obtain it you must get Emily to reach the friendship level of 7 hearts.

Ingredients
(1) Radish
(1) Red Cabbage

Information
Buff(s) Max Energy (+50)
Buff Duration 3m 30s
Energy 240
Health 108
Sell Price 400g
