Blackberry Cobbler

There's nothing quite like it

Blackberry Cobbler is a cooked dish. You can prepare this dish in the kitchen of your home when you have the upgraded farmhouse.

This recipe is unlocked by watching The Queen of Sauce on TV on the 14th day of Fall in Year 2.
It can also be randomly given as a gift at the Feast of the Winter Star festival that will take place on the 25th of every Winter.

Ingredients
(2) BlackBerry
(1) Sugar
(1) Wheat Flour
